3. Never Giveup

no matter what the situation is one should keep going,struggling but never stoping. its ok to feel tired and take a day or two off but whats not ok is to just stop there.
you might feel like irony that i'm talking about never giving up when its me who is always on the verge of giving up.
let me tell you something about myself. whenever i'am learning anything, first it goes very well,it feels like i'am super intelligent but soon enough when some advance topics start coming up my learning slope goes down and down daily.then i'am like how can i be soo dumb. i guess it happens with everyone. sometimes you feel like you know everthing but also feel like nah..i no nothing.
at that time we have two options. one is close the book, close your laptop, leave that subject and go far as possible. basically give up. and second option is think of why you started, and go back a few topics and do them again. at this point of my life what i understood is that its ok to not be able to understant some topics in one go or maybe as quickly as others. its completely alright to find yourself doing somthing 2 times. but what matters is your attitude of not giving up.
here are few things i do when i get stuck and subject feels too heavy:
-go back few topics and do it again -search for a different source, maybe youtube or someones blog -take a break of 1 or 2 days and come back again.
i hope it helps,and just want to say like dont giveup, keep going,may be slower then others but atleast you are moving forward.


2. Data Structure

i was learning DS as a part of my MCA syllabus and thought to write some blogs on data structure one topic at a time. from basic begninning to the end of difficult part. this blog is about baisc of ds
firslty what are data structure? they are basically arrangement or collection of data so that they can be used efficiently.
things to keep in ming-Arrangment and Operation and inside main memory. every application deals with some data, that data has to be in main memory. how can we arrange them for efficient use. that arrangement is called data structure.
array,matrices, linkedlist are physical data structure-how arranges they define how memory should be organized during storing of data
stacks,queues,trees,graph,hashing,recurrsion,sorting are logical data structure-implemented using array and linked list
some terms to keep in mind: databse-how data is organised in harddisk in some model like relational model. coomertial data- it can be categorized into 2 parts operational- which is used currently legacy data/old data - kept as storage somewhere so that is can be used later. so the large size data which is kept is called data warehouse.storing and analysing very large size data, that study is called big data
